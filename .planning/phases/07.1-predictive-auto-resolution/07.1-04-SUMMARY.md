## Decisions Made
- SVG overlay approach for bracket accuracy coloring: imports layout constants from BracketDiagram, renders a transparent SVG positioned absolutely on top. This avoids modifying the base BracketDiagram component and follows the 04-04 overlay pattern.
- Dense ranking for student leaderboard: scores with identical totalPoints + correctPicks share the same rank number, preventing misleading "you're #15" when several students are tied.
- Countdown skipped for Round 1: the first reveal starts immediately without a 3-2-1 countdown, since there's no prior round to build suspense from.
- Server-side prediction fetch triggers when PredictiveStudentView detects revealing/completed status, using Promise.all for parallel fetch of predictions and scores.
- Podium auto-dismisses after 12 seconds (vs CelebrationScreen's 10s) because the staggered 3-entry entrance takes ~1.5s before all entries are visible.

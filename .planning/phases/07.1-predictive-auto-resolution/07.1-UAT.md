## Tests

### 1. Predictive Resolution Mode in Bracket Creation
expected: When creating a predictive bracket, Step 2 shows three resolution mode options including "Predictive" (alongside Manual and Vote-based). Selecting it and proceeding to Step 3 shows it in the review summary.
result: pass

### 2. Prepare Results (Auto-Tabulation)
expected: After predictions close on an auto-mode predictive bracket, the teacher can click "Prepare Results" on the live dashboard. The system tabulates all student predictions as majority votes, filling in winners for every round automatically. A preview bracket diagram appears showing the computed winners.
result: pass

### 3. Override a Matchup Winner
expected: On the preview screen, teacher can click any matchup to open an override modal. The modal shows both entrants with their prediction vote counts. Selecting an alternate winner and confirming updates the bracket diagram, including downstream matchups that are affected by the change.
result: issue
reported: "Override modal for downstream matchups shows stale entrant data. After overriding a quarterfinal matchup (e.g. choosing Vaccines over Computer), the bracket diagram correctly updates the semifinal to show Printing Press vs Vaccines, but clicking that semifinal matchup to override shows the original entrants (Printing Press and Computer) instead of the updated ones (Printing Press and Vaccines). Same issue observed with other overrides (e.g. Printing Press instead of Lightbulb)."
severity: major

### 4. Release Results
expected: After reviewing the preview, teacher clicks "Release Results". A confirmation dialog appears. After confirming, the bracket transitions to revealing state and the first round of results becomes visible.
result: issue
reported: "Confirmation dialog appears correctly. However, after confirming, the first round is NOT revealed. Instead the button changes to 'Reveal Next Round' — teacher must click again to reveal what is actually round 1."
severity: minor

### 5. Progressive Round Reveal (Teacher)
expected: Teacher sees a "Reveal Next Round" button. Each click reveals the next round of winners on the bracket diagram. A 3-2-1 countdown animation plays between rounds (except Round 1 which shows immediately). After all rounds are revealed, the bracket shows as completed.
result: issue
reported: "Progressive reveal works for rounds after the first, but once the final round is revealed and bracket is completed, the Prediction Leaderboard and Prediction Stats sections render twice (duplicated below the bracket diagram)."
severity: minor

### 6. Presentation Mode
expected: Teacher presses the F key (or clicks a presentation button) to enter full-screen presentation mode with a dark overlay background. The view toggles between bracket diagram and leaderboard. Pressing F or Escape exits presentation mode.
result: issue
reported: "Presentation mode works during the reveal phase but becomes unavailable once the bracket is marked completed. No F key toggle or presentation button on the completed state."
severity: minor

### 7. Student Waiting State
expected: While the teacher is preparing/previewing results (before release), students see a waiting state with a spinner and message like "Your teacher is preparing results..." rather than a blank or broken page.
result: pass

### 8. Student Progressive Reveal
expected: When the teacher reveals rounds, students see the bracket fill in progressively in real time. Each matchup shows an accuracy overlay: green highlight for correct predictions, red highlight (with strikethrough) for incorrect predictions.
result: issue
reported: "Student view does live update progressively with accuracy overlay (green/red highlights), but the accuracy badge on each bracket card shows raw ASCII escape text '\\u27' instead of the intended icon/checkmark."
severity: minor

### 9. Student Animated Leaderboard
expected: Students see a leaderboard tab showing the top 10 scorers plus their own rank. As rounds are revealed and scores update, leaderboard entries animate smoothly to their new positions. Tied scores share the same rank number (dense ranking).
result: pass

### 10. Podium Celebration
expected: When all rounds are revealed (bracket complete), a podium celebration appears showing the top 3 students with gold/silver/bronze styling and confetti. The podium auto-dismisses after about 12 seconds.
result: issue
reported: "No podium celebration appears. Final round reveal just shows brackets and leaderboards and the bracket as closed."
severity: major

### 11. LiveDashboard Auto-Mode Routing
expected: When viewing an auto-mode predictive bracket on the live dashboard, SE-style controls (round tabs, action buttons) are hidden. Instead, the PredictiveBracket component renders with its own lifecycle controls appropriate to the current state.
result: pass

### 12. Reopen Predictions
expected: While in the previewing state (before releasing results), teacher can click a "Reopen Predictions" button to go back to predictions_open. This clears all computed winners and lets students modify their predictions.
result: issue
reported: "Reopen Predictions button works on teacher side. However, after teacher clicks 'Close Predictions', students can still navigate to 'Edit Results' and change/resubmit their predictions. The student UI does not enforce the closed state."
severity: major

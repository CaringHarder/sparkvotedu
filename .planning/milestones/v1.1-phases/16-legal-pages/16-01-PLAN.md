**Privacy policy content sections (draft content appropriate for an EdTech platform):**

1. **Introduction** -- SparkVotEDU is a classroom engagement platform for teachers and students. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. **Information We Collect**
   - *Teacher accounts:* Name, email address, password (hashed), subscription/payment info (processed by Stripe -- we do not store card numbers).
   - *Student participation:* Students join sessions anonymously using a class code. We assign a random fun name (e.g., "Dancing Penguin"). We do NOT collect student names, emails, or any personally identifiable information from students.
   - *Device fingerprinting:* We use anonymous device fingerprinting solely to prevent duplicate voting within a session. This data is not linked to any individual identity and is stored locally on the device.
   - *Usage data:* Standard web analytics (page views, feature usage) to improve the service.

3. **How We Use Your Information**
   - To provide and maintain the service
   - To process teacher account registration and subscriptions
   - To prevent duplicate voting (device fingerprinting)
   - To improve the platform based on usage patterns
   - To communicate with teachers about their accounts

4. **Student Privacy (COPPA & FERPA Considerations)**
   - SparkVotEDU is designed with student privacy as a core principle. Students participate anonymously -- no student accounts, no student PII collected.
   - Device fingerprinting is used only for session integrity (preventing duplicate votes) and is not used to track or identify students.
   - Teachers control all classroom content and participation.
   - We do not sell, share, or monetize any student data.

5. **Third-Party Services**
   - *Supabase* -- authentication and data storage
   - *Stripe* -- payment processing for teacher subscriptions
   - *Vercel* -- hosting and content delivery
   - Each service has its own privacy policy. We only share the minimum data required for each service to function.

6. **Data Security**
   - We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS) for all data in transit.
   - Teacher passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text.
   - Access to data is restricted to authorized personnel.

7. **Data Retention and Deletion**
   - Teacher account data is retained while the account is active.
   - Teachers may request account deletion by contacting us, which will remove all associated data.
   - Anonymous student session data is retained for classroom analytics and may be deleted by the teacher at any time.

8. **Your Rights**
   - Access, correct, or delete your personal information
   - Cancel your subscription at any time
   - Request a copy of your data
   - Contact us with privacy concerns

9. **Changes to This Policy**
   - We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify teachers of significant changes via email.

10. **Contact Us**
    - Email: privacy@sparkvotedu.com
    - For questions about this privacy policy or our data practices.

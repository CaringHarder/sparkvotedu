<feature>
  <name>Double-Elimination Bracket Engine</name>
  <files>src/lib/bracket/double-elim.ts, src/lib/bracket/__tests__/double-elim.test.ts</files>
  <behavior>
  Pure functions. No DB calls.

  generateDoubleElimMatchups(size: number) -> DoubleElimMatchups:
  Returns { winners: MatchupSeed[], losers: MatchupSeed[], grandFinals: MatchupSeed[] }

  For size=4 (2 WB rounds):
  - Winners: 3 matchups (same as single-elim for 4)
    - R1P1: seed 1 vs 4, R1P2: seed 2 vs 3, R2P1: winners meet
  - Losers: 2 rounds (LB has 2*(log2(N)-1) = 2 rounds for N=4)
    - LB R1 (minor): 1 matchup (WB R1 losers play each other)
    - LB R2 (major): 1 matchup (LB R1 winner vs WB R2 loser)
  - Grand Finals: 1 matchup (WB champion vs LB champion)
  - Total: 3 + 2 + 1 = 6 matchups

  For size=8 (3 WB rounds):
  - Winners: 7 matchups (same as single-elim for 8)
  - Losers: 4 rounds (2*(3-1) = 4)
    - LB R1 (minor): 2 matchups (4 WB R1 losers -> 2 matchups)
    - LB R2 (major): 2 matchups (2 LB R1 winners + 2 WB R2 losers)
    - LB R3 (minor): 1 matchup (2 LB R2 winners -> 1 matchup)
    - LB R4 (major): 1 matchup (LB R3 winner + WB R3 loser)
  - Grand Finals: 1 matchup
  - Total: 7 + 6 + 1 = 14 matchups

  All matchups must have:
  - round (relative to their bracket region)
  - position (1-indexed within the round)
  - nextMatchupPosition (links to next matchup in same region, or null for region final)
  - Winners bracket matchups use region-local round numbers (R1, R2, R3)
  - Losers bracket matchups use region-local round numbers (LBR1, LBR2, LBR3, LBR4)
  - Grand finals round = 1, position = 1

  seedLosersFromWinnersRound(wbLosers: string[], lbRoundSize: number) -> string[]:
  - Reverses the order of losers from a WB round to avoid rematches in LB
  - For >2 losers: uses split-and-reverse (split at midpoint, reverse each half, concatenate second-half first)
  - For 2 losers: simple reverse
  - For 1 loser: identity (single element)
  </behavior>
  <implementation>
  1. generateDoubleElimMatchups:
     - Winners bracket: call existing generateMatchups(size) from engine.ts
     - Losers bracket: Build iteratively. Track LB round number and field size.
       - LB R1 (minor): size/4 matchups (WB R1 losers play each other)
       - Then for each remaining WB round (2 through log2(size)):
         - Major round: currentFieldSize matchups (LB survivors vs WB dropdowns)
         - Minor round: currentFieldSize/2 matchups (if >1 remaining)
     - Grand Finals: single matchup with null seeds (filled by WB and LB champions)
     - All losers bracket matchups have entrant1Seed and entrant2Seed as null (filled dynamically during advancement)

  2. seedLosersFromWinnersRound:
     - For length <= 2: return reversed
     - For length > 2: split at midpoint, reverse each half, return [...secondHalf, ...firstHalf]
  </implementation>
</feature>

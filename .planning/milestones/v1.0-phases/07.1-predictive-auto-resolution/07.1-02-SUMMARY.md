## Decisions Made
- Mode-specific transition maps (MANUAL vs AUTO) selected at runtime by bracket.predictiveResolutionMode, rather than a single combined map that would allow invalid cross-mode transitions
- Auto-open matchups for voting restricted to vote_based mode only -- auto mode resolves via tabulation, not voting rounds
- Override triggers full downstream clear and re-tabulation rather than incremental patch -- simpler, more reliable, and the operation is rare
- Reveal validates sequential order (must reveal round N+1 after round N) to prevent skipping rounds during classroom presentation
- Reopen clears all non-bye winners and all propagated entrants for rounds > 1, providing a clean slate for re-prediction
